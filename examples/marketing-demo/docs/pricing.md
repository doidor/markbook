## What counts as "a service"?

Any long-running workload. A web server is a service. A background worker is a service. A scheduled job that fires every five minutes is a service. Static sites, edge functions, and one-shot tasks are free across every plan.

## Egress

Egress to anywhere on the public internet is **$0.02/GB** beyond the first 100GB/month included on every plan. Egress between Cumulus services — including to your own object storage — is always free.

## Volume

Above 5TB of storage or 10TB of egress per month, talk to us. We meet or beat AWS/GCP rate cards in writing, and we'll commit to it for the term of your contract.
