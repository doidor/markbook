## Case studies

### Lattice — Replatformed three legacy clouds in a quarter

Lattice had services scattered across AWS, GCP, and a regional bare-metal provider. They consolidated onto Cumulus over twelve weeks, reduced operational headcount on infrastructure by 40%, and cut their monthly bill by $180k.

### Outshelf — From "Kubernetes is our full-time job" to "I forgot we had infra"

A 40-person startup, Outshelf had two engineers spending most of their week tending to a Kubernetes cluster. After moving to Cumulus, both of them returned to product work full-time. They now deploy 14 times a day on average.

### Plumcove — Compliant from day one

Plumcove needed SOC 2, HIPAA, and ISO 27001 to land their first enterprise customer. Cumulus's inherited compliance posture let them check every control without writing a single piece of infrastructure code. They closed the deal six weeks earlier than planned.
